1. Gayatri Mantra

om bhurbhuvah svah,
tatsaviturvarenyam
bhargo devasya dhimahi
dhiyo yo nah prachodayat ॥

2. Mahadev

om trambakam yajamahe,
sugandhim pushtivardhanam
urvarukamiv bandhanan
mrutyormukshiya maamrutat ॥

3. Shri Ganesha

vakratund mahakay,
surya koti samaprabha
nirvignam kuru me dev,
sarvakaryeshu sarvada ||

4. Shri hari Vishnu

mangalam bhagavan vishnuh,
mangalam garunadhvajah.
mangalam pundari kakshah,
manglay tano harih॥

5. Shri Brahma ji

om namaste paramam brama,
namaste paramatne .
nirgunay namastubhyam,
saduyay namo nam ||

6. Shri Krishna

vasudevasutam devam,
kansachaanooramardanam.
devki paramanandam,
krushnam vande jagadgurum ||

7. Shri Ram

shri ramay rambhadray,
ramchandray vedhase .
raghunathay nathay,
sitaya pataye namah ||

8. Maa Durga

om jayanti mangla kali,
bhadrakali kapalini .
durga kshama shiva dhatri,
svahaa svadha namo̕stute ||

9. Maa Mahalakshmi

om sarvabadha vinirmukto,
dhan dhanyah sutanvitah .
manushyo matprasaden,
bhavishyati na sanshayahom ||

10. Maa Saraswathi

om sarasvati namastubhyam,
varade kamrupini.
vidyaarambham karishyami,
siddhirbhavatu me sada ||

11. Maa Mahakali

om kreen kreen kreen,
haleen hreen khan sphotay,
kreen kreen kreen phat ||

12. Hanuman ji

manojavam marutatulyavegam,
jitendriyam buddhimatam varishtham.
vatatmajam vanarayuthamukhyam,
shriramdutam sharanam prapadye॥

13. Shri Shanidev

om nilanjanasamabhasam,
raviputram yamagrajam .
chhayamartandsambhutam,
tam namaami shanaischaram ||

14. Shri Kartikeya

om sharavana-bhavaaya nam:,
dnyanashaktidhara skanda ,
valleeekalyana sundara.
devsena man: kanta,
kartikeya namostute ||

15. Kaal Bhairav ji

om hrim vaam batukaye,

kuru kuru batukaye,
hrim batukaye svahaa.||
